Once a Brother, Always a Brother

Welcome to the Brotherhood 🎉

Your profile is approved — here's everything your membership unlocks, in two minutes.

1

Polish your profile

Click your name in the top-right corner → My Profile. Add a photo, your grad year, city, occupation, and how brothers can reach you. The more you fill in, the more useful the brotherhood directory becomes — especially for mentoring and referrals.

Open my profile →
2

Find yourself in the Family Tree

Every brother since 1993 is on the tree — big to little, all ten founding lines. Use the Find a brother search to jump straight to your line, and click any brother to see his full profile (that's a members-only privilege you now have).

See the tree →
3

Put yourself on the map

The Brotherhood Worldwide is a live map of where brothers are today — actives and alumni, one pin per city, from Geneseo to everywhere the brotherhood went. Add your current city in My Profile and your pin appears.

See the map →
4

The private Gallery

An Instagram-style photo feed only verified brothers can see. Post pictures from the house, reunions, or the group chat archives — like and comment on everything.

Open the gallery →
5

The Board — including jobs & referrals

Four spaces: Chapter Business, Advice, Social, and Opportunities — where brothers post jobs and internships they're offering or ask for referrals they're seeking. One connection here pays for the whole network.

Visit the board →
6

Events & the directory

The events calendar lists reunions, philanthropy, rush and homecoming — some events are members-only, so check while signed in. The Active and Alumni directories let you filter brothers by class year, city, and profession.

🔔

Stay in the loop

The bell in the header lights up when brothers like or comment on your posts, reply to your threads, or when chapter leadership has news. Questions or something looks wrong? Use the contact form — it goes straight to the webmaster.

Once a brother, always a brother. Glad you're here. — ΖΒΞ
